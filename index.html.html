About the Organizer: NIH

The **National Institute of Hydrology (NIH)** is a premier R&D organization under the Ministry of Jal Shakti, Government of India, established in 1978.

Its main objectives are to undertake, aid, promote, and coordinate systematic and scientific work in all aspects of **hydrology and water resources**. NIH is an ISO 9001:2008 Certified Center of Excellence, focusing on technology transfer and demand-driven, user-defined, strategic research.

*Sponsored by National Water Mission.*

What You Will Master

Climate Science Fundamentals

  • Climate change impacts on water resources
  • **CMIP6, GCMs, RCMs,** and **CORDEX** frameworks
  • Reanalysis Data (IMDAA, ERA5, MERRA-2)

Hands-on Data Tools (Coding)

  • Pre-processing data with **CDO**
  • Visualize climate data using **GrADS**
  • Data handling with **NumPy & Pandas**
  • NetCDF Data processing with **Xarray**

Advanced Techniques

  • Dynamical & Statistical Downscaling methods
  • **AI & ML** in Climate Science applications (Part 1 & 2)
  • Climate Data Services of **IMD**

DIY & Certification

  • Build your own climate data workflow (**DIY Project**)
  • Project Presentation and Peer Learning
  • Official Certificate of Participation
  • Complimentary **Field Visit**
